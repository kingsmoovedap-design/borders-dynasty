# Dynasty OS — Treasury Constitution

The Treasury Constitution defines the principles, rules, and flows that govern payouts, credit, rewards, and financial transparency.

---

## ARTICLE I — TRANSPARENCY
All treasury flows must be visible and auditable.

---

## ARTICLE II — PREDICTABILITY
Payout rules must be deterministic and consistent.

---

## ARTICLE III — FAIRNESS
Drivers, partners, and the Dynasty receive clear shares.

---

## ARTICLE IV — STABILITY
Treasury maintains operational reserves.

---

## ARTICLE V — REWARDS
Performance and loyalty are rewarded.

---

## ARTICLE VI — INTEGRATION
Treasury integrates with:
- Dispatch  
- Compliance  
- Codex  
- Credit  
- Marketplace  

---

## ARTICLE VII — ACCOUNTABILITY
All treasury actions are logged in Codex.
